Taiwan Student Association

Welcome

The Taiwan Student Association, also know as TWSA, is an student cultural organization at the University of Michigan created by students from Taiwan. For the past 8 years, we have hosted countless events and help students find their own community to truly make the University of Michigan their home away from home.

Who We Are

TWSA is a community that centers around Taiwanese culture. Our mission is to share Taiwanese culture with everyone on the UM campus. TWSA's doors is always open to anyone from any culture!

What We Do

We host programs and monthly events. There is something going on every month! Sometimes we even have multiple events per month. The majority of our events are open to all!

Mentorship Program

The Mentorship Program is something that is very important to TWSA. Every Fall semester, we pair up incoming freshmen or transfer students with a student who has been a UM student for at least a year. Any question you may have, whether it is about classes, living in the US, social activities, your mentor is there to help! We also have monthly mentorship events for mentors and mentee to bond.
