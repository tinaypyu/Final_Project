Events

TWSA organizes multiple events every year! The following are some of the events that TWSA has had in the past.

  • See events from 2023
  • See events from 2022

2023 Events

2023 Mahjong Tournament

A TWSA well-loved tradition. After three rounds of intense competition, the final winner gets a sash that they get to keep for an entire year!

2022 Events

2022 Mahjong Tournament

A TWSA well-loved tradition. Also the first one that we had in the US after Covid. After three rounds of intense competition, the final winner gets a sash that they get to keep for an entire year!

TWSA's Single Inferno

Valentine's Day for single people. Of course, couples are also very welcomed! We had a series of valentine-themed games for everyone to enjoy and a prize in the end for the winners.

Movie Night

A great way to take a break from your studies a destress. We watched famous scary movie based on real historical events in Taiwan.

April Tomb Sweeping Day Event

We collaborated with the Hong Kong Student Association (HKSA) to organize an event for Tomb Sweeping Day. Players had to beat games and answer trivia questions in order to get ingredients for spring roll, an classic Tomb Sweeping Day dish.

Class of 2026 Freshmen Info Session

Going to a foreign country alone to study can be scary, but TWSA is here to help! We tell you what to expect and answer any and all question that you may have.

Freshmen Dinner

Usually takes place over the summer in Taiwan. This is a great opportunity for freshmen to meet each other and other UM studetns from Taiwan.

Mass Meeting

Curious about TWSA? Mass meeting is for you! Learn about what TWSA is and what we do. Also meet TWSA members and potential TWSA members.

Foodie

TWSA's event for Autumn Festival, which is traditionally a time when family comes together and spend time with each other. Since we are all far from home, we had some fun together on the Hub deck.

Mental Wellness Workshop

Recruiting season can be very, very stressful, and that's why a little self care is important. In a focus group led by a CAPS counselor, we talked about stress and ways to cope with it.

Winter Wonderland Masquerade Party

An end of year, christmas themed event before everyone starts winter break. Participants chatted and ate dinner while making masquerade masks. We also watched football!
